Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using the KnowIt learning platform

  1. Click the "Register" button in the navigation menu (sidebar or hamburger menu on mobile)
  2. Fill out the registration form with:
    • Full Name: Your complete name
    • Username: Choose a unique username
    • Email: A valid email address
    • Phone Number: Your contact number (optional)
    • Password: A secure password
  3. Click "Register" to create your account
  4. You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to the homepage
Note: All new accounts are created with "Student" role by default. Contact an administrator to change your role to Teacher.

To Login:
  1. Click the "Login" button in the navigation menu
  2. Enter your username and password
  3. Click "Login" to access your account
  4. You'll be redirected to the homepage with your personalized dashboard
To Logout:
  1. When logged in, you'll see a "Logout" button in the navigation menu
  2. Click the "Logout" button
  3. You'll be logged out and redirected to the homepage
Security Tip: Always logout when using shared computers or when you're done with your session.

There are several ways to return to the home page:

  • Click the "Know It" logo in the top navigation bar - this works from any page
  • Use your browser's back button to navigate back through your browsing history
  • Type the homepage URL directly in your browser address bar
Quick Tip: The "Know It" logo in the header changes color when you hover over it and always takes you back to the home page!

For Students:

  1. Login to your student account
  2. Click "Browse Courses" in the navigation menu
  3. Browse through the available courses created by teachers and admins
  4. Click "View" on any course you're interested in
  5. On the course page, click the "Enroll Now" button
  6. The course will appear in your "My Enrolled Courses" section
Managing Your Enrollments:
  • View enrolled courses: Click "My Enrolled Courses" in the menu
  • Unenroll from a course: Click "Unenroll" button on the course page or in your courses list
  • Access course content: Click "View" on any enrolled course
Note: Only students can enroll in courses. Teachers and admins create and manage courses but don't enroll in them.

Student
  • Browse available courses

  • View course content

Teacher
  • Create new courses
  • Edit their own courses
  • View enrolled students

Admin

  • Edit any course
  • Change user roles
  • All platform permissions

For Teachers and Admins:

  1. Login with your teacher or admin account
  2. Go to "My Courses" in the navigation menu
  3. Click the "Add New Course" button
  4. Fill out the course form:
    • Title: A unique, descriptive course title
    • Description: Brief overview of the course
    • Content: The main course material and lessons
  5. Click "Create Course" to save
  6. Your course will be available for student enrollment
Note: You need Teacher or Admin role to create courses. Contact an administrator to upgrade your account.

Can't login?
  • Check your username and password for typos
  • Make sure Caps Lock is off
  • Clear your browser cache and try again
Don't see "Add Content" option?
  • Check your user role - only Teachers and Admins can create courses
  • Contact an administrator to upgrade your account
Can't enroll in a course?
  • Make sure you're logged in as a Student
  • Check if you're already enrolled in the course
  • Try refreshing the page
Page not loading properly?
  • Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
  • Clear your browser cache
  • Try using a different browser
  • Check your internet connection

If you can't find the answer you're looking for, contact our support team.
